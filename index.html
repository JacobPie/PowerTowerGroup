Our Product

The Power Tower Group is a team of engineers and computer scientists who are on a mission to make truly wireless charging a reality. And we don’t mean the “wireless chargers” that force you to place your phone on a pad in order for it to receive power, we mean standing 3 meters away from the charging station and having your device charge without wires or cables.

At the moment, the Power Tower has not been developed.

  • Superior charging distance
  • Able to charge from up to three meters away
  • More power efficient than competitors
  • Uses less energy than competitors
  • 100% safe to use in any room
  • Kid-friendly and safe
